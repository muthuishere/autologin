General questions

Why AutoLogin?

Each and everyday we would be going to a site which would looks up for credentials. Autologin by capturing credentials for the first time allows you to login automatically to the site if its going to prompt.

Is my data safe?

All your data is encrypted with AES 256 and stored with in your browser. All data resides only within your Browser.

How its different from saved passwords from browser?

Saved Passwords will only fill the credentials on the site, You have to manually click the submit button to login to the site. Autologin fill the crdentials and clicks the button for you as well.
Autologin Settings

How to Goto Settings?

  • On Google Chrome
    • Goto chrome://extensions/
    • Lookup for Autologin
    • Click Options link
  • On firefox
    • Goto chrome://extensions/
    • Click Master Password ->Change Button
    • Enter Password and confirm again
    • Click Save

How to setup master password

  • Goto options
  • Click on Settings
  • Click Master Password ->Change Button
  • Enter Password and confirm again
  • Click Save
