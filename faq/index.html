AutoLogin FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

General questions

Why AutoLogin?

People visit websites that require credential entry on a daily basis. AutoLogin, by remembering your information, allows you to login automatically to these websites, saving your time in the process.

Is my data safe?

Your data is encrypted with AES 256 and stored with in your browser. All data resides only within your browser.

How is it different from passwords saved by the browser?

Saved passwords will only fill the credentials on the site. You still have to manually click the submit button to login. AutoLogin fills the credentials and clicks the button for you as well.

AutoLogin Settings

How to go to settings?

  • Click menu -> More tools -> extensions
  • Look for AutoLogin
  • Click Options link

How to set up a master password?

  • Go to settings
  • Click Master Password -> Change Button
  • Enter password and confirm again
  • Click Save
